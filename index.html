MISSION

To advance women in all aspects of Somaliland society by consolidating and strengthening their collective power

VALUES

EQUALITY OF OPPORTUNITIES

A thriving Somaliland powered by men and women with equal access to leadership and justice.

INCLUSIVE MOVEMENT

We aspire to have a broad-based movement driven by women from all parts of society.

SELF-RESPECT

We need women to respect and believe in themselves. They should believe that they can excel in all parts of the government.

LOCATION

Hiil Movement is based in Somaliland, an unrecognized country in the horn of Africa. Somaliland shares borders with Djibouti in the northeast, Ethiopia in the south and west, and Somalia in the south. Somaliland declared its independence after the removal of dictator Siyad Barre of Somalia in 1991. In the late 1990s, Somaliland leaders and citizens voted on creating a representative democratic state with local elections in 2002, presidential elections in 2003, and parliamentary elections in 2005. Somaliland has a population of about 5 million people, its own flag, government, and constitution.
